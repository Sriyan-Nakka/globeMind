# Globe Mind 🌎

Welcome to Globe Mind, my most modern-looking and most varied game yet! This is a game which tests your geography skills and how much information you know about countries.

You main goal is to guess the answer to a country-related question in the time limit you have selected. If you fail to do so, you lose.

You can answer/guess various questions about countries such as:
- Guess the Country by its flag
- Guess the Capital of the Country by its flag
- Guess the Continent of the Country

And so on!

You either get to play in short-bursts of 10 flags per match in **Casual** Mode, or you can guess forever until you can't guess the answer in **Endless** Mode! Try it out at https://globe-mind.netlify.app
