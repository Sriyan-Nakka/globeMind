Globe Mind

How to Play

Welcome to Globe Mind, the game that tests your geography skills!

When you press the Start button, you will be presented with a series of modes which you have to select. Modes are the different ways you can challenge your geography skills.

For example, You chose "Guess the Country by Flag" mode. In this mode, you will be shown a Flag and you have to guess the country of the flag in the selected time limit. If you guess it right, you get to move on to the next country. This same pattern continues depending on which mode you have chosen.

Game Types:
There are different methods of playing this game called 'Game Types'. The first type is called 'Normal', where you have to guess 10 answers in the selected time. The second type is 'Endless', where you have to keep guessing answers depending on your mode until you fail to guess the correct answer.

Unable to guess the answer to the question? You can click the Hint button to reveal a letter in the answer. You start off with 3 hints, and you gain 3 hints after guessing 10 answers in Endless Mode.

You can guess how many answers as you want in the selected time limit in any Game Type. But, if you fail to guess the answer correctly in that time, you lose immediately. Hope you like it! :)

Note: Answers to questions are based on Country Lookup API.
